A Bit More Info

My Story

I grew up in Brazil with very itchy feet, I couldn't wait to be able to travel and discover the world. Through hard-work and dedication I got accepted to Hunter College in New York City where I graduated with Honors. I have called US home for over a decade now.

My Work

Working with the web for the past 10 years. The titles have changed (web designer, web animator, UI designer) but my focus never has. I create engaging interfaces and intuitive interactions, while learning and keeping up with this amazing new medium.

I initially worked on-site but soon moved to working remotely where I discovered that I excel at organizing my own time, predicting the length of production, and communicating with and updating the team. This gave me the opportunity to work with a multitude of agencies, mostly helping with projects on a monthly if not weekly basis. I'm proud to say that those relationships have all lasted for many years.

My client list includes: Disney, Antieau Gallery - New Orleans, Honda, Marvel, Chicago on Broadway and many others.

Please feel free to get in touch if you are interested in working together.
