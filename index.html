My Story

I grew up in Brazil with very itchy feet, I couldn't wait to be able to travel and discover the world. Through hard-work and dedication I got accepted to Hunter College in New York City where I graduated with Honors. I have called US home for over a decade now.

My Work

I always strive for engaging and thoughtful design, kick-ass animation and semantic, light & clean code.

I started working with the Web in 2007 and over these past 10 years I have developed my skills in HTML, CSS, Sass, jQuery & Wordpress.

My projects come from individuals and large marketing agencies.

My client list includes: Disney, Antieau Gallery - New Orleans, Honda, Marvel, Chicago on Broadway and many others.
