They’re young talented and hungry for success. Welcome to POP NATION, the new musical experience that puts you behind the scenes as boy bands and bubblegum divas blast off on a whirlwind ride to the heights of pop royalty.

A social theatrical experience unlike anything you’ve ever seen, POP NATION is a groundbreaking new genre set in the iconic 90’s music scene that explores what it’s like to rise, fall, and rise again in a world increasingly more concerned with the sight — rather than the sound — of its latest pre-packaged superstars.
