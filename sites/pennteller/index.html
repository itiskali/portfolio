Marquis Theatre

46th St. between Broadway & 8th Ave.

PENN & TELLER seem to be everywhere: Celebrity Apprentice, Dancing with the Stars, world tours, Las Vegas! Now, the evil geniuses return to the place that helped launch their careers: Broadway! For the first time in almost 25 years, the duo comes home, where they will bring astonishing tricks from their record-breaking Vegas act, along with their most outrageous classics, to the Marquis Theatre.

Throughout their eclectic and indefinable careers, the duo has stabbed and shot at each other, been run over by tractor trailers, and caught bullets in their mouths. Now, for 6 weeks only, PENN & TELLER promise to shock and amaze Broadway like never before, and keep it all family friendly…for really demented families.
